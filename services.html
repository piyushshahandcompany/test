Our Professional Services

Piyush Shah and Company offers a comprehensive suite of Chartered Accountant services designed to support businesses, professionals, and institutions at every stage of their lifecycle. Our services combine regulatory expertise, commercial insight, and disciplined execution.

SME Advisory Services

Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) are the backbone of the Indian economy, contributing significantly to employment generation, exports, and industrial growth. SMEs account for nearly 40% of India’s exports and employ a substantial portion of the workforce. However, SMEs often face challenges related to compliance, access to finance, taxation complexity, and financial discipline.

At Piyush Shah and Company, we provide end-to-end advisory services to SMEs, helping them establish strong financial foundations and scalable compliance systems. Our services include accounting system design, tax planning, GST compliance, cash flow management, audit support, and regulatory filings.

We work closely with SME promoters to ensure statutory compliance while enabling informed decision-making through accurate financial reporting and MIS analysis. Our approach is practical, proactive, and aligned with long-term business sustainability.

Finance & Business Advisory

Whether a business intends to raise capital, expand operations, acquire another entity, or realise value through restructuring or exit, sound financial advisory is critical. Strategic decisions require robust financial analysis, compliance awareness, and risk evaluation.

Our finance and advisory services cover financial planning, capital structuring, investment evaluation, due diligence support, and strategic advisory. We assist clients in preparing financial projections, business plans, and investor-ready documentation.

With deep experience across industries, we help businesses align financial strategy with regulatory frameworks and commercial objectives, ensuring clarity, compliance, and confidence at every stage of growth.

Government Grants & Subsidies Advisory

Government grants and subsidies play an important role in promoting economic development in a developing country like India. Various central and state government schemes are designed to support MSMEs, exporters, startups, and manufacturing units.

We assist clients in identifying eligible schemes, preparing documentation, and ensuring compliance with subsidy conditions. Our services include application filing, certification, liaison support, and post-sanction compliance.

By leveraging our regulatory understanding, we help clients optimise benefits under government incentive schemes while ensuring transparency and compliance.

Tax Advisory & Compliance

Increased regulatory scrutiny, frequent amendments, and extensive reporting requirements have made tax compliance complex and time-consuming. Effective tax advisory requires not just compliance, but strategic planning.

At Piyush Shah and Company, we offer dedicated income tax and GST advisory services, including tax planning, return filing, assessments, appeals, and representation before tax authorities.

Our approach focuses on lawful tax efficiency, risk mitigation, and proactive compliance management to protect client interests while ensuring regulatory adherence.

Corporate Affairs & Company Law Services

Corporate compliance under the Companies Act, 2013 and allied regulations requires continuous monitoring and timely filings. Non-compliance can result in penalties and governance risks.

We provide end-to-end corporate law advisory including company incorporation, ROC filings, director compliance, restructuring, and regulatory advisory.

Our corporate affairs services ensure robust governance frameworks, statutory discipline, and smooth regulatory interactions.
