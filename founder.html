Professional Leadership Built on Trust

CA Piyush Shah is a seasoned Chartered Accountant and the Founder of Piyush Shah and Company, a well-established professional firm based in Masjid Bunder, Mumbai. With more than two and a half decades of hands-on experience, he has advised businesses, professionals, and institutions across taxation, audit, compliance, and strategic financial matters.

Since establishing the firm in 1996, CA Piyush Shah has built a reputation for integrity, technical depth, and disciplined professional execution. His practice reflects a Big-4 style approach—structured, compliance-driven, and aligned with global best practices—while maintaining the accessibility and personal attention of an independent firm.

Experience & Expertise

Over the years, CA Piyush Shah has developed extensive expertise in Income Tax, GST, audit and assurance, corporate law compliances, accounting, and business advisory services. His experience spans multiple industries, including manufacturing, trading, services, MSMEs, and family-owned enterprises.

He is particularly recognized for handling complex tax matters, regulatory assessments, audit engagements, and compliance frameworks. His advisory approach is practical and risk-focused, enabling clients to make informed decisions while remaining compliant with Indian laws and regulations.

As a Chartered Accountant practicing in Mumbai with PAN India service capability, CA Piyush Shah regularly advises clients located across Maharashtra and other states, delivering consistent professional standards irrespective of geography.
